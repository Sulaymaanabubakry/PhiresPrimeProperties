Our Simple 5-Step Process

From inquiry to land ownership - we make it easy

1

Choose Your Plot

Browse our available estates and select a plot that matches your budget and preference. Our team provides detailed information about each location.

2

Schedule Site Inspection

Visit the location with our representative. We organize free site inspections to help you make an informed decision about your investment.

3

Make Initial Payment

Pay the initial deposit (typically 10% of total cost) to secure your plot. We offer flexible payment plans from 6 to 12 months.

4

Complete Payment

Complete your payment according to the agreed schedule. We send regular reminders and receipts for every payment made.

5

Receive Your Documents

Get your verified title documents including Deed of Assignment, Survey Plan, and Receipt. We also assist with the perfection process.
