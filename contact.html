Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I start the land purchase process?

Simply browse our available projects, select your preferred location, and contact us to schedule a free site inspection. Our team will guide you through every step.

What documents will I receive?

You'll receive a Deed of Assignment, Survey Plan, Receipt of Payment, and the relevant title document (C of O, Excision, or Gazette) depending on the property.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans from 6 to 12 months with an initial deposit of just 10% of the total cost.

Are your titles genuine and verified?

Absolutely! All our properties come with 100% verified and genuine title documents. We conduct thorough due diligence on every property we offer.

Can I visit the site before making payment?

Yes! We encourage site inspections and offer FREE site visits with our representatives. This helps you make an informed decision.

What is the minimum plot size available?

Plot sizes vary by location, typically ranging from 300sqm to 600sqm. We can also accommodate special requests for larger plots depending on availability.

How long does it take to process documents?

Once full payment is made, we typically process and deliver your documents within 30-45 days. For payment plan clients, documents are issued after final payment.

What are the benefits of early investment in your estates?

Early investors enjoy discounted prices, best plot selection, and watch their investment appreciate as infrastructure develops and property values increase.

Can I resell my property after purchase?

Yes! Once you have full ownership and documents, you're free to resell or develop your property. We can also assist with connecting you to potential buyers.

Are there any hidden charges or fees?

No hidden charges! All costs including survey, documentation, and legal fees are transparently communicated upfront. What you see is what you pay.
